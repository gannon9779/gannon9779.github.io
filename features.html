Why us

We’re not a franchise.
We’re your neighbors.

This page goes deeper into what makes Nittany STEM Tutoring a fit for State College families: we’re a consultancy built around STEM outcomes, near-peer teaching, and local context—not generic worksheets or outsourced tutors.

01
Pure STEM focus

We don’t do everything — we do STEM. That specialization means our tutors know these subjects deeply, not just well enough to get by. Every session is taught by someone who’s recently survived the same courses you’re in.

02
Near-peer tutoring works

Penn State students teaching Penn State-bound (and beyond) students. We remember what it was like to not get it. We explain things the way a sharp older classmate would — no condescension, no over-simplified garbage.

03
Accountability built in

We track progress session to session and flag patterns before they become problems. If a student keeps missing the same type of problem, we don’t move on. We fix the root issue.

04
Pricing that makes sense

$50/hour for 1-on-1 is below market for this area. We keep it that way on purpose — students shouldn’t have to choose between consistent help and affordability. Group sessions drop that to $25 or less.

05
Local. Actually local.

We’re based in State College. We know the school curriculum, the teachers, the pace of SCASD and surrounding schools. We’re not a national platform farming out sessions to whoever’s available.

06
Real understanding. Not shortcuts.

We could teach you to pass tomorrow’s test with tricks. We don’t. Shortcuts catch up with students in the next course, on the SAT, in college. We teach concepts that stick, because that’s actually useful.

The process

Simple. Structured. Consistent.

How we run the consultancy day to day: you’re not buying a mystery package—you’re getting a clear path from first conversation to steady progress.

1
Book & brief call

Reach out by email or the contact form. We’ll ask a few quick questions about your student — subject, grade level, what’s not clicking — and match you with the right tutor. No intake form the length of a lease agreement.

2
Personalized study plan

First session, we assess where things stand and build a plan. Not a generic template — an actual plan based on what this specific student needs right now.

3
Weekly sessions

Regular, consistent sessions at a set time. Consistency is what makes tutoring work. We keep sessions focused and cover ground efficiently — no time-wasting.

4
Progress tracking

We track what was covered and where improvement is happening. If grades aren’t moving, we adjust the approach. You’ll know exactly where things stand, every step of the way.
