From the founder: why I built this tutoring site

I didn’t start Nittany STEM Tutoring because I thought tutoring was a fun side hustle. I started it because I kept seeing the same gap—students with plenty of motivation and supportive parents, still stuck in classes that move fast and assume you already “speak math.”

At Penn State, my peers and I live in labs, problem sets, and exam weeks. We remember the feeling of being one confused step behind while the lecture speeds up. The national apps and generic centers I tried as a model didn’t feel like State College: they didn’t line up with SCASD pacing, local exam styles, or the jump from high school STEM to what PSU expects.

So I built something smaller and more honest: a consultancy where tutors are accountable for explanation quality, not just credentials on a flyer. Where we track patterns session-to-session instead of resetting every week. Where pricing is clear because families shouldn’t need a spreadsheet to know if help is sustainable.

If you’ve ever paid for help that graded your kid instead of teaching them, you know why this matters.

If that story resonates, the next step is simple: visit the site, skim services & pricing, and either book a kickoff or reply with your student’s grade and subject. We’ll tell you honestly if we’re the right fit.

— The Nittany STEM team
