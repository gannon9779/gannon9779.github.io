About

Started by Penn State students who got tired of bad tutoring options.

Nittany STEM Tutoring was built around a simple observation: most tutoring services are either too expensive, too generic, or staffed by people who learned the material years ago and forgot what it’s like to be confused by it.

We’re Penn State students tutoring in subjects we’re actively studying. We know what SCASD teachers emphasize. We know how AP Calculus is graded. We know what intro coding actually looks like in high school versus college.

Every tutor goes through a vetting process — being smart enough to do the math isn’t enough. You have to be able to explain it. That’s a different skill, and it’s the one that matters for your student.

We keep our group sizes small, our prices honest, and our focus narrow. STEM only. State College first. That’s the whole deal.
